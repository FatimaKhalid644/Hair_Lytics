Care Instructions

Master the art of hair maintenance with our expert directions.
Follow these steps to maintain hair health and get the best results from your products.

Daily Care Routine

Shampooing

  • Focus application only on the scalp area.
  • Use lukewarm water to open hair cuticles.
  • Massage gently with fingertips, never nails.
  • Rinse thoroughly until water runs clear.

Conditioning

  • Apply from mid-lengths to hair tips.
  • Strictly avoid applying on the scalp.
  • Leave for 2-3 minutes for penetration.
  • Rinse with cold water to seal cuticles.

Oil Treatment

  • Section hair and apply oil to the scalp.
  • Use circular motions to boost blood flow.
  • Leave for 30 minutes before washing.
  • Wash with lukewarm water for best results.
Massage Techniques

Circular Scalp Massage

  • Apply light pressure using your finger pads.
  • Move in small circular motions across the head.
  • Reduces stress and promotes hair growth.

Inversion Stimulation

  • Lower your head slightly to increase blood flow.
  • Massage for 4-5 minutes daily.
  • Helps oxygen reach the hair follicles.
Natural Home Remedies

Aloe Vera Mask

  • Extract fresh gel and mix with 1 tsp honey.
  • Apply to scalp for a cooling effect.
  • Wash after 30 minutes for hydrated hair.

Protein Boost

  • Mix egg white with 2 tablespoons yogurt.
  • Apply to hair lengths for structural repair.
  • Rinse with cool water to prevent odor.
